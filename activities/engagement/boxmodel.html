Engagement Questions

1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags? Block tags have height and widht, inline tags dont have any of this. they have surround text. div, header, nav, footer are block tags, h1-h6, p, ul, ol, li tags are also block tags. span, em strong, b, i, a, img tags are an example of inline tags.

2. What is the margin, border, and padding? Margin is the space outside of the elements border. Padding is when you want to push away something inside of the box. Border is the edge of the element.

3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS? One of the examples are px, thin, inches, cm, these are all fixed sized. %, em these are examples of relative sized.

4. When would you use a figure tag? I can use figure tags when I will make sure that images are grouped.
