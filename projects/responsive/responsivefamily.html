Meet my family

This is my dad. He is a very friendly and kind person. He always love to serve other people as he work. He served his mission in the Philippines Manila Mission. He is currently serving at our stake as a stake high council member. He always gives everything we need

She didn't serve a mission, but she is currently serving in the relief society presidency for our ward. I love my mom.

This is my older brother. Me and my brother are opposites. He loves to cosplay and watch anime, I love to play sports and go outside. He served his mission in the Philippines Cagayan De Oro Mission. He is very talented and hardworking as well. He loves serving the youth in our stake.
