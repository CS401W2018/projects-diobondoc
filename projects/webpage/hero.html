Dragon Knight

Dragon Knight is a strong and straightforward hero who can contribute to his team in many ways. His tankiness allows him to be a frontliner, while his crowd control and burst potential from Elder Dragon Form make him a force to be reckoned with in both early and late game teamfights.

Factions
Dragon Knight is part of the Scaled Knights of Uthorian. This faction is ancient where all dragon knights of the world reside.
Powers
Elder Dragon Form is Dragon Knight's ultimate ability, which transforms him into a more powerful dragon. When activated, his attack range increases, and he gains bonus damage, making him deal significant physical damage in fights.
Weaknesses
