Who is Mirana?

In Dota 2, Mirana is a popular ranged agility hero known for her versatile abilities and strong presence in the game. She is often played in both core and support roles. Mirana is a demigoddess and is tied to the moon, which is reflected in her abilities and lore.

These are her following abilities:

  • Starstorm
  • Sacred Arrow
  • Leap
  • Moonlight Shadow

Mirana's versatility and ability to fit into multiple roles make her a beloved hero in both casual and professional Dota 2 games. Her ability to both deal damage and provide utility makes her a strong pick in many different team compositions.
